The Just Powers Foundation is a nonprofit think tank dedicated to promoting individual liberty, free markets, and constitutional governance through research, policy analysis, and public education.

## Our Mission
We believe that a free society depends on the rule of law, respect for individual rights, and economic freedom. Our work focuses on:

- Conducting original research on policy issues affecting liberty
- Publishing analyses that inform public debate
- Educating citizens about constitutional principles
- Advocating for limited government and individual responsibility

## Our Approach
Our research is grounded in rigorous analysis and draws from economics, law, history, and philosophy. We maintain strict independence from political parties and special interests, ensuring our work serves the public interest.

## Leadership
The foundation is led by a team of scholars and policy experts with extensive experience in academia, government, and think tanks.
