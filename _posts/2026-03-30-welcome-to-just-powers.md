Welcome to the official website of the Just Powers Foundation! We're excited to launch our online presence and share our work with you.

## Who We Are

The Just Powers Foundation is a new nonprofit think tank dedicated to advancing liberty through rigorous research and policy analysis. Our mission is to promote the universal principles of just powers as articulated in the Declaration of Independence, recognizing that free markets are their natural consequence.

## What We Do

Our foundation will focus on:

- **Original Research**: Conducting in-depth studies on policy issues affecting liberty
- **Policy Analysis**: Providing clear, evidence-based recommendations for reform
- **Public Education**: Making complex ideas accessible to policymakers and citizens
- **Advocacy**: Promoting principles of limited government and individual responsibility

## Our First Publications

We're currently working on our inaugural research reports covering:

- Just powers principles and their universal application
- The economics of government spending and debt
- Property rights in the digital economy

Stay tuned for these publications, which will be available for download on our [Research page](/research/).
